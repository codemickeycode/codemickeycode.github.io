About

I build systems that align tools and technology with human needs. I help ideas move from intent to impact, working through uncertainty and limited resources to reach ambitious goals.

I’m a software engineer turned ecosystem builder, working at the intersection of technology, education and social impact.

As Program Coordinator of Ateneo TRL (Technical Resource Lab), I help shape efforts around digital public infrastructure (DPI), building open, people-centered systems that make government services more inclusive and accessible.

As Director of Operations at Python Philippines (PythonPH), I build sustainable, volunteer-driven programs and financial models that keep community work alive and independent.

Python has been at the center of my journey — not just as a programming language, but as a community I’ve chosen to serve. Guided by open source values, I care deeply about women in tech, and economic empowerment through skills and access.
