About

I’m a software engineer turned ecosystem builder, working at the intersection of technology and social impact. As Program Coordinator of the Technical Resource Lab (TRL) at Ateneo, I co-lead efforts to advance Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)—reimagining identity, payments, and data exchage systems so they serve the public in more inclusive and accessible ways.

I also serve as Director of Operations at Python Philippines (PythonPH) and am a Python Software Foundation Fellow, where I’ve spent years nurturing grassroots leadership and building sustainable, volunteer-driven systems.

At my core, I use my Python background and open source values to catalyze change—bridging technology, community, and impact. I like to defy the odds for the things I truly care about:

  • Open Source
  • Women in Tech
  • Economic empowerment through skills and access
  • Building technology that puts people first
