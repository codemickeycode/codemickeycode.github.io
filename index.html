About

I build systems that make technology and tools truly serve people. I help ideas move from intent to impact, even when starting in ambiguity, with limited resources and ambitious outcomes.

I’m a software engineer turned ecosystem builder, working at the intersection of technology and social impact. My work spans open source, education, and govtech — translating vision into systems that actually work.

As Program Coordinator of Ateneo TRL (Technical Resource Lab), I help shape efforts around digital public infrastructure (DPI) in the Philippines, helping make systems more inclusive and interoperable.

As Director of Operations at Python Philippines (PythonPH), I build sustainable, volunteer-driven programs and financial models that keep community work alive and independent.

Python has been at the center of my journey — not just as a programming language, but as a community I’ve chosen to serve. Guided by open-source values, I care deeply about women in tech, economic empowerment through skills and access.
